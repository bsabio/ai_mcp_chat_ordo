# The Thread: Sources and Intellectual Lineage

## What This Book Is

This book documents the intellectual foundation of the Second Renaissance Knowledge System — the people, research, frameworks, and design traditions this curriculum draws from.

Every claim in the curriculum rests on evidence. Every framework traces to people who did the work before us. This book makes that trace visible.

## How to Use It

Each chapter documents one luminary or tradition: who they are, what they found or built, the primary works, and exactly where their ideas appear in the curriculum so you can follow the influence forward.

Use this book to:
- Understand the evidence base behind a specific claim before teaching or presenting it
- Find primary sources for deeper reading on any topic in the curriculum
- Build the intellectual lineage into course design and syllabi
- Verify that claims are accurately represented before repeating them to others
